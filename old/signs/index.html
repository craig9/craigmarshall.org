Carved Wooden Signs

Between 2009 and 2011, I learned how to carve wooden signs by hand, using a mallet and gouges. I built a small online business, selling my signs to people all across the UK. I managed to just about make a living doing this for a year or so, stopping when I left the UK to move to Tasmania.
